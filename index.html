Concept is a showcase of WCI's talent

We are a collaboration of drama, dance and music performances. Put together by the students of WCI, we are a show that offers a wide variety of entertainment. Join us November 27, 28, and 29 in the WCI auditorium. Ticket sales start November 20, $5 in advance, $6 at the door. Bring your wild side.
